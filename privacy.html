Privacy policy

Effective 16 July 2026

What we store

The beta stores a signed anonymous browser session, encrypted Higgsfield OAuth tokens, generation prompts, model choices, job status and returned output links. OAuth tokens remain server-side.

Provider processing

Generation requests and reference URLs are sent to Higgsfield under the account you connect. Higgsfield's own terms and privacy practices apply to that processing.

Security and retention

Tokens are encrypted using deployment secrets. Do not submit confidential material during public beta. Generation history may be retained to provide the gallery and troubleshoot failures.
